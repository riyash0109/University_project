VISION & MISSION

"CHRIST (Deemed to be University) is a nurturing ground for an individual's holistic development to make an effective contribution to the society in a dynamic environment."

A ture Christite

Goal Driven

One of the most significant qualities of a christite is that he/she is goal-driven. Having both short- and long-term goals will assist in achieving your objectives

Perseverance

Perseverance is synonymous with dedication. Christite will not quit up and accept incompetence, no matter how complex the subjects/courses are. Instead, Christite will always put in sufficient effort, if not more, to produce excellent output or performance.

Balance

A good Christite is capable of being well-rounded. That is to say; christite always remember that they have other responsibilities in life, such as being a child, a sibling, or a friend they can still assist with housework, spend time with your family, and meet up with friends on occasion

Responsible

One of the qualities of a christite, as well as a good performer, is responsibility. They never blame their mistakes or flaws on excuses; instead, they accept full responsibility for their acts and accept the consequences of their actions. Good chrsitite also take responsibility for developing their character while in school so that they can graduate with successful capabilities
